Time Calculator

The Time Calculator lets you add or subtract two time durations expressed in hours, minutes, and seconds. It also includes a time conversion tool that converts any time value between seconds, minutes, hours, and days. This tool is useful for tracking work hours, calculating event durations, scheduling, and any situation where you need to perform arithmetic on time values.

How to Use the Time Calculator

  1. For Add/Subtract: Enter hours, minutes, and seconds for both time values, select the operation, and click Calculate.
  2. For Time Conversion: Enter a value, select the unit you are converting from, and click Convert to see the equivalent in all other units.
  3. Results automatically handle carrying over — for example, 75 minutes becomes 1 hour 15 minutes.

Understanding Time Results

When adding time, minutes and seconds are automatically carried over into the next unit when they exceed 59. When subtracting, if the result would be negative, the calculator shows the absolute difference and notes the direction. The conversion tool shows the equivalent value in seconds, minutes, hours, days, and weeks simultaneously.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add time durations?

To add time, convert everything to seconds, add the totals, then convert back. For example, 1h 30m + 45m = 90 min + 45 min = 135 min = 2h 15m. Our calculator does this automatically.

How many seconds are in an hour?

There are 3,600 seconds in one hour (60 seconds × 60 minutes). There are 86,400 seconds in one day (3,600 × 24).

How do I calculate total work hours?

Enter your start and end times as two time values and use the subtract operation. For example, if you worked from 9:00 AM (9h 0m 0s) to 5:30 PM (17h 30m 0s), subtract to get 8 hours 30 minutes.

How many minutes are in a day?

There are 1,440 minutes in one day (60 minutes × 24 hours). There are 10,080 minutes in one week.
