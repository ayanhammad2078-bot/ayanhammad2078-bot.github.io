Whether you need to convert miles to kilometers, pounds to kilograms, Fahrenheit to Celsius, or gallons to liters, this tool handles it all accurately and without any external dependencies.

How to Use the Unit Converter

  1. Select a category from the dropdown (Length, Weight, Temperature, Volume, or Speed).
  2. Choose the unit you are converting from in the "From" dropdown.
  3. Choose the unit you are converting to in the "To" dropdown.
  4. Enter the value you want to convert.
  5. Click Convert to see the result instantly.

Supported Unit Categories

Length: Millimeter, Centimeter, Meter, Kilometer, Inch, Foot, Yard, Mile.
Weight: Milligram, Gram, Kilogram, Metric Ton, Ounce, Pound, Stone.
Temperature: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin.
Volume: Milliliter, Liter, Cubic Meter, Teaspoon, Tablespoon, Cup, Pint, Quart, Gallon.
Speed: m/s, km/h, mph, Knot, Mach.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, use the formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32. For example, 100°C = (100 × 9/5) + 32 = 212°F. Our converter handles this automatically when you select the Temperature category.

How many kilometers are in a mile?

1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. Conversely, 1 kilometer = 0.62137 miles. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions for travelers between countries using metric and imperial systems.

What is the difference between mass and weight?

Mass is the amount of matter in an object (measured in kilograms or pounds), while weight is the force of gravity acting on that mass. In everyday use, the terms are used interchangeably, and our converter handles common mass/weight units.

How many liters are in a gallon?

1 US gallon = 3.78541 liters. Note that the US gallon and the UK (imperial) gallon are different — 1 UK gallon = 4.54609 liters. Our converter uses the US gallon standard.
