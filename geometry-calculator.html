How to Use the Geometry Calculator

  1. Select a shape from the dropdown menu.
  2. Enter the required dimensions (radius, side lengths, height, etc.).
  3. Click Calculate to see the area, perimeter, volume, or surface area.
  4. Results are displayed with clear labels and explanations.

Supported Shapes

2D Shapes: Circle (area, circumference), Rectangle (area, perimeter), Triangle (area, perimeter).
3D Shapes: Sphere (volume, surface area), Cylinder (volume, surface area), Cone (volume, surface area).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the formula for the area of a circle?

The area of a circle is A = πr², where r is the radius. For example, a circle with radius 5 has an area of approximately 78.54 square units.

How do I find the volume of a sphere?

The volume of a sphere is V = (4/3)πr³, where r is the radius. Our calculator computes this automatically when you select the Sphere shape.

What is the difference between area and perimeter?

Area measures the space inside a 2D shape (in square units), while perimeter measures the distance around the outside of the shape (in linear units).

How do I calculate the surface area of a cylinder?

The surface area of a cylinder is SA = 2πr² + 2πrh, where r is the radius and h is the height. This includes the area of both circular bases and the curved side.
