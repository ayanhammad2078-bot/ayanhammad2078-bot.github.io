Age Calculator

The Age Calculator computes your exact age in years, months, and days based on your date of birth. It also tells you how many days are left until your next birthday. This tool is useful for filling out forms, verifying eligibility requirements, or simply satisfying your curiosity about your precise age down to the day.

Please enter a valid date of birth (not in the future).

How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Enter your Date of Birth using the date picker.
  2. The Calculate Age As Of field defaults to today's date. You can change it to any date to calculate age at a specific point in time.
  3. Click Calculate Age to see your exact age in years, months, and days.
  4. The result also shows the total number of days you have been alive and days until your next birthday.

Understanding Your Age Result

Your age is calculated by finding the difference between your date of birth and the selected date. The result breaks down into complete years, remaining months, and remaining days. The total days alive is calculated as the exact number of calendar days between the two dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is age calculated exactly?

Age is calculated by counting complete years from the date of birth to the reference date, then counting remaining complete months, and finally the remaining days. This gives a precise breakdown of your age.

Can I calculate someone else's age?

Yes. Simply enter the person's date of birth and set the "Calculate Age As Of" field to today or any other date. The calculator works for any person, past or present.

What if I was born on February 29 (leap year)?

The calculator handles leap year birthdays correctly. In non-leap years, the birthday is treated as March 1 for the purpose of calculating the age transition.

Can I use this to check legal age requirements?

Yes. You can enter a date of birth and check the age as of today to verify whether someone meets a minimum age requirement (e.g., 18 or 21 years old).
